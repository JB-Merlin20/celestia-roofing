Overview

Roofing Built for South Florida Homes

Your roof is the single biggest line of defense between your family and everything South Florida can throw at it — relentless sun, torrential rain, salt air, and the occasional hurricane. At Celestia Crest, we build residential roofs the way we would build them for our own families: with the right materials, the right details, and the right people on the ladder.

We have installed, replaced, and repaired thousands of roofs across Coral Gables, Pembroke Pines, Plantation, Homestead, Dania Beach, North Miami Beach, Opa-locka, and surrounding communities. Every project starts with a free, honest inspection and ends with a home that looks better, drains better, and performs better for decades to come.

The reason most homeowners hesitate to call a roofer is because they've been burned before — bait-and-switch quotes, no-shows, surprise change orders, and crews that leave nails all over the driveway. We built Celestia Crest specifically to fix those problems. Our estimators show up on time, our quotes are itemized and honest, and our crews treat your home the way they would treat their own mother's.

We serve homeowners across the full price spectrum — from starter homes in Homestead and Opa-locka to luxury estates in Coral Gables and Pembroke Pines. Every homeowner gets the same honest inspection, the same transparent pricing, the same careful craftsmanship, and the same post-project follow-through. Price points vary. Quality does not. Whether you are spending $8,000 or $80,000, you are getting our A-team, our best materials for that system, and the same workmanship warranty that has earned us hundreds of five-star reviews.

Every residential roof we install is registered with the manufacturer and backed by our own written workmanship warranty. If an issue ever arises, you call us, we come out, we fix it. No runaround, no pointing fingers at the manufacturer, no 'that's not covered.' Our goal is to be the last roofer you ever need — and we earn that position one honest project at a time.

Frequently Asked

Questions We Get Asked

How long does a residential roof replacement take?

Most homes are completed in 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. We always give you a clear timeline before we start.

Will my homeowner's insurance cover the work?

If your roof was damaged by a covered peril (wind, hail, storm), we help document the claim and work directly with your adjuster.

What kind of warranty do I get?

Workmanship warranties of 10-25 years plus manufacturer material warranties up to lifetime, depending on the system you choose.

Do you protect landscaping during the work?

Yes. We tarp all landscaping and A/C units, run magnetic nail sweeps daily, and clean the site thoroughly before we leave — every day, not just at the end of the job.

Can I stay home during the roof replacement?

Yes, most homeowners stay home during the work. It is noisy during tear-off, but you and your pets can remain inside safely throughout the project.
