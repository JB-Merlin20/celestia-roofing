Flat & Low-Slope Roof Systems

Flat roofs are common on Florida mid-century homes, additions, sunrooms, and commercial buildings. They require very different installation techniques than pitched roofs -- and a contractor who actually knows what they are doing.

Celestia Crest installs and replaces every common low-slope system: SBS modified bitumen, TPO single-ply membranes, and traditional built-up gravel roofs. We focus on proper drainage, fully sealed seams, and reinforced flashing at every penetration.

Common Flat Roof Issues We Solve

  • Ponding water after heavy rain
  • Seam separation and blistering
  • Aged sealant around HVAC and vent penetrations
  • Interior leaks despite a "dry-looking" roof surface
