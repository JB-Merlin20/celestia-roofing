About Us

A Roofing Company Built on Trust & Craftsmanship

Since 2010, Celestia Crest has protected thousands of South Florida homes and businesses with roofs engineered to outlast hurricanes, sun, and salt air.

Our Story

Local Roofers, Generational Standards

Celestia Crest Roofing Contractors began with a simple promise: deliver the kind of roof we'd want over our own families' heads. More than fifteen years later, we've completed 2,400+ projects across seven South Florida cities -- from historic Coral Gables tile restorations to brand-new metal installs in Pembroke Pines.

Every crew member is in-house, trained on the systems we install, and equipped to handle Florida's most demanding conditions. We don't subcontract your roof to the lowest bidder; we hire, train, and stand behind our own team.

From your first call to the final inspection, you'll work with people who answer the phone, show up on time, and clean the job site before they leave.
