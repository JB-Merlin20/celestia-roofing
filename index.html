About Celestia Crest

Craftsmanship You Can See From the Curb

Celestia Crest Roofing Contractors has been protecting South Florida homes and businesses since 2010. Our team specializes in every roofing type -- from classic barrel tile to standing-seam metal and modern flat systems. We understand the unique challenges that Florida weather brings, and we engineer every roof to stand up to hurricane-force conditions.

Serving Coral Gables, Pembroke Pines, Plantation, Homestead, and neighboring cities, we have built our reputation on transparent pricing, clean job sites, and roofing work that lasts decades. Every project comes with a written warranty and a commitment to your complete satisfaction.
